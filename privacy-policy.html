Privacy Policy

Greetings from the Jaya Madhva Trust website. Our Privacy Policy is crafted to provide you with insights into how we handle the collection, utilization, and disclosure of personal information gathered from users visiting our site.

Information Gathering:

Users may willingly share personal information, such as names, email addresses, and contact details, when engaging with our website.

Utilization of Information:

The data we collect serves various purposes, including:

Enhancing Website Functionality: The information collected ensures the proper functioning and continuous improvement of our website, tailored to meet the needs and expectations of users.

Responding to Inquiries: When users reach out to us, the provided information is employed to respond to inquiries and furnish the necessary details.

Improving User Experience:

Understanding user preferences and behaviors empowers us to elevate the overall user experience on our website.

Communication:

The information may be used to share updates, newsletters, and communications related to events and activities organized by Jaya Madhva Trust.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

In order to gain insights into user interactions with our website, we employ cookies and similar tracking technologies. These tools assist in analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking user movements, and collecting demographic information. Users retain the option to manage their cookie preferences at the browser level.

Information Safeguarding:

While we implement commercially reasonable measures to protect personal information, it is imperative to acknowledge that absolute security cannot be guaranteed. Users are urged to take personal precautions to safeguard their information.

Links to Third-Party Websites:

Our website may include links to third-party sites. Users should be cognizant that we do not control the content or privacy practices of these external sites. It is advisable for users to review the privacy statements of other websites they choose to visit.

Policy Updates:

This Privacy Policy may undergo updates without prior notice. Any modifications will be promptly posted on this page to keep users informed about the collection, utilization, and circumstances under which their information may be disclosed.

Contact Information:

For any inquiries or clarifications regarding this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at prabhu.pamnetwork@gmail.com.

In essence, Jaya Madhva Trust remains dedicated to upholding the privacy of our users and ensuring transparency in the collection, utilization, and protection of their personal information. We encourage users to periodically review this Privacy Policy for any updates or alterations.
