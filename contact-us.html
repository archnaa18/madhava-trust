Keep In Touch

As stated earlier since most of the people have lost faith in Trusts and NGOs in general due to mis-deeds we felt the best way is to take the donor to the schools in the villages directly and meet up with the children, identify their requirements and deliver it directly.

What we can do is give the prospective donors the list of such schools with number of children in each school and approximate expenses towards each of the school. The donor could communicate the name of the school and then we could take the donor and meet the children as well as the headmaster and local panchayat (village leaders) so that funds will not misused. Further, Trust will provide all supporting bills towards the money donated by the donor. For further details in Kannada ( Local Language).
