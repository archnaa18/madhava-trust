About Us

As dreampt by Jaya Madhava, the Trust instituted in her name viz., Jaya Madhava Trust was to initially help 60 children 6 children of each standard from 1st standard to 10th standard. But when we met the school children in a remote village it was extremely difficult to choose who was poor among them. Hence it was decided to support all the children of the School. We selected 4 such schools and finally supported 172 children instead of initial plan of 60 children.

Vision:

The Trust has a vision to add atleast 60 children each year for the next few years to reach 600 numbers within the next few years.

Objectives of the trust

  • To help school going children in the age group of 1st standard to 10th standard by providing them with School bags, shoes, uniforms, books and other stationaries requires for study purposes.
  • To provide Libraries with general knowledge books, story books etc., to enhance the knowledge of school going children.
  • To impart job-oriented computer education.
  • Providing free food to children’s homes on certain occasions.
  • Providing to children at children’s homes with School bags, shoes, uniforms, books and other stationaries requires for study purposes.
  • Provide funds to temple for Puja, renovation, construction, Anna Dhana (on shashwatha Puja basis) etc.,
  • Provide funds for food, prasad in the temple.
